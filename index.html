About

A researcher who builds things with hands, not just models

I hold a PhD in Computer Science with a focus on artificial intelligence, and lead a data science & AI team at a fintech company working on credit and BNPL products. Day-to-day I work across ML systems, LLM integrations, ClickHouse & PostgreSQL pipelines, and Kafka-based data infrastructure.

Outside the office, I design and build analog circuits — guitar effects, tube amplifier stages, and synthesizer modules. I write firmware for ESP32 and Daisy Seed microcontrollers with a focus on real-time audio DSP. The same signal-processing intuition that informs my ML work shows up in how I think about filter design and harmonic generation.

I play electric guitar in the spirit of David Gilmour — long, sustained notes over carefully crafted effects chains. Some of those effects I built myself.
